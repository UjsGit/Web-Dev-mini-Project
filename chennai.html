About Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital of Tamil Nadu and one of India's major cultural, economic, and educational centers. With a rich history dating back to the British East India Company in the 17th century, Chennai has evolved into a vibrant metropolis while preserving its traditional charm.

The city is famous for its Marina Beach, historic temples, colonial architecture, and being the hub of South Indian classical music and dance. It's also known as the "Detroit of India" due to its thriving automobile industry.
