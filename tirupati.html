About Tirupati

Tirupati is a renowned spiritual city located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. Home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, it attracts millions of pilgrims annually, making it one of the most visited religious sites in the world.

The city is nestled in the beautiful Tirumala Hills, part of the Eastern Ghats range, and combines spiritual significance with natural beauty. Beyond its religious importance, Tirupati is also known for its rich cultural heritage, traditional arts, and delicious local cuisine.
