About Mysore

Mysore, officially Mysuru, is a historic city in Karnataka known for its glorious royal heritage and magnificent monuments. The city served as the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore for nearly six centuries.

Famous for its silk sarees, sandalwood products, and the grand Mysore Palace, the city offers a perfect blend of traditional architecture, art, culture, and modern amenities. The Dasara festival celebration here is world-renowned.
