About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, formerly known as Bangalore, is the capital of Karnataka and India's leading IT hub. Known as the 'Garden City' for its beautiful parks and pleasant climate, it perfectly blends modern technology with traditional charm.

The city is famous for its tech parks, startup ecosystem, beautiful gardens, historic palaces, and vibrant nightlife. It's also a major center for India's aerospace and biotechnology industries.
