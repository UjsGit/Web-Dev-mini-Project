About Vellore

Vellore is a historic city in Tamil Nadu, known for its magnificent 16th-century Vellore Fort and the renowned Christian Medical College. The city perfectly blends its rich historical heritage with modern educational excellence and healthcare facilities.

Once the seat of the Vijayanagara Empire, Vellore has witnessed numerous historical events and still retains its cultural significance. Today, it's also an important educational hub and medical tourism destination in South India.
