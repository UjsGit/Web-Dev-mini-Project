About Kurnool

Kurnool, the former capital of Andhra Pradesh, is a historic city with rich cultural heritage. Located at the confluence of the Tungabhadra and Handri rivers, it serves as the gateway to the Rayalaseema region.

The city is known for its historical forts, caves, and religious sites. It's also a significant agricultural and educational center in the region, blending its historical significance with modern development.
