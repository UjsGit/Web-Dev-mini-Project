About Vijayawada

Vijayawada, situated on the banks of the Krishna River, is one of the largest cities in Andhra Pradesh. Known for its historical importance and modern development, it serves as a major commercial and cultural hub.

The city is famous for its ancient caves, temples, and the Prakasam Barrage. It's also an important center for agriculture, business, and education in the region.
