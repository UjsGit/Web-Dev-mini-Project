About Coimbatore

Coimbatore, also known as Kovai, is a major industrial city in Tamil Nadu. Known as the "Manchester of South India" for its textile industry, the city is surrounded by the Western Ghats and offers a perfect blend of industry, culture, and nature.

The city is famous for its pleasant climate, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and its proximity to popular hill stations. It's also a major hub for textile manufacturing, engineering, and information technology.
